# ElectroNet Project Journey

## Project Overview
This document chronicles the development journey of the ElectroNet project, from initial conception through prototype testing and final presentation preparation.

---

## Timeline of Events

### November 15, 2025 - Project Inception
**Team Formation and Initial Planning**

- Formation of the ElectroNet research team
- Creation of project coordination Telegram group
- Initial idea conceptualization and framework discussion
- Team Lead: Yusuf
- Mentor: Jamalulkhair
- Academic Supervisor: Teacher Wan Norliza

**Research Foundation**

Yusuf (team lead) initiated the project by sharing foundational research papers:

1. **Primary Research Paper:**
   - [Science Direct - Electrocoagulation Study](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1674237023001217)
   - Focus: Water treatment methodologies

2. **Supporting Research:**
   - [ACS Langmuir Journal](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.langmuir.3c01700)
   - Focus: Surface chemistry and electrochemical processes
